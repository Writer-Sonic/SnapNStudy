# Snap AI Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Snap AI app.

Snap AI helps users photograph questions, generate worked solutions, and continue learning with AI-assisted study tools. To provide that service, we process some account information, content you choose to submit, and limited technical data needed to operate the product.

We do not sell your personal information and we do not display third-party ads inside the app.

## Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

- **Identifiers and account information** such as your unique account ID and, depending on how you sign in, your email address, name, or similar account details.
- **User content** such as photos you upload, questions you type, tutor chat messages, audio you submit for transcription, saved solutions, study history, subjects, and preferences.
- **Contact information** if you choose to contact us through support from inside the app.
- **Purchase and entitlement information** such as subscription status, plan details, and whether your account currently has access to paid features.
- **Diagnostics and technical data** such as device type, operating system, app version, timestamps, IP address, crash or error details, and related service logs. Some diagnostics may be collected without being linked to your identity.
- **Attribution information (iOS only, optional)** such as a limited Apple Search Ads attribution token collected via Apple's AdServices framework, used solely to understand which Apple Search Ads campaign brought a new user to the app. This is described in detail in the *App Tracking Transparency* section below. macOS does not collect attribution data.

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- create and manage your account;
- process photos, questions, audio, and follow-up requests;
- generate answers, explanations, and study tools through our backend service and third-party data-processing services;
- save your library, history, settings, and preferences across sessions and devices;
- provide subscriptions, credits, and paid features;
- measure the effectiveness of Apple Search Ads campaigns and improve onboarding (iOS only, when you grant App Tracking Transparency permission);
- respond to support requests and feedback submitted through the app;
- monitor performance, troubleshoot problems, prevent abuse, and keep the service secure; and
- comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

## How Information Is Shared

We may share information with service providers that help us operate Snap AI, such as providers that support account access, hosting, storage, billing, diagnostics, customer support, AI or other data processing on our behalf, and Apple Search Ads attribution measurement (iOS only, when you allow tracking).

We may also disclose information:

- if required by law, legal process, or valid governmental request;
- to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users, our service, or the public; or
- as part of a merger, acquisition, financing, or similar business transaction.

We do not sell your personal information.

## AI and Content Processing

When you choose to use AI-powered features, the content you submit, such as photos, text questions, chat messages, and audio, may be sent to our backend service and trusted third-party data-processing services so Snap AI can generate answers and study guidance.

You can review or withdraw AI data-processing consent from within the app where that option is available.

## App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS, Snap AI uses Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework to ask permission before collecting any data that could be used to track you across other companies' apps and websites.

- If you select **Allow**, Snap AI together with Apple's AdServices framework and our subscription provider RevenueCat may receive Detailed Apple Search Ads attribution data (such as campaign, ad group, keyword, and ad identifiers) so we can understand which marketing campaign introduced you to the app.
- If you select **Ask App Not to Track** or skip the prompt, only Standard attribution data (no advertising identifier, no detailed campaign-level information) is collected.
- Your photos, scanned questions, tutor chats, audio, study history, and account content are never used for tracking and are never sold or shared with advertisers regardless of your ATT choice.
- The ATT prompt only appears on iOS. macOS users are not tracked.

You can change this setting at any time in iOS **Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking**.

## Subscriptions, Purchases, and Web Purchase Redemption

Subscription state, in-app purchases, and entitlements are handled through Apple's StoreKit and our subscription infrastructure provider RevenueCat. If you complete a purchase on the web (for example via a Stripe-backed checkout offered through our paywall), RevenueCat issues a one-time redemption link that securely associates the web purchase with your in-app account on iOS. The redemption flow does not transmit any new personal information beyond what is required to recognize and grant the purchase.

If you purchase a subscription or other in-app product on iOS or macOS, payment information is processed by Apple and we do not directly store your full payment card information. For web purchases, payment is processed by Stripe (or a comparable PCI-compliant payment provider) and we do not directly store your full payment card information either.

## Advertising

Snap AI does not display third-party ads inside the app and does not use third-party ad networks to serve ads to you. The only advertising-related processing is the optional Apple Search Ads attribution measurement described in the *App Tracking Transparency* section above, which lets us understand whether new users came from an Apple Search Ads campaign — it does not show you ads.

## Data Retention

We keep information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, maintain account functionality, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and protect the platform.

If you delete your account from within the app, we delete the main study content associated with that account from our app storage, including your profile, saved solves, notebooks, chat history, uploaded photos, and saved AI memory data.

Some limited records may be retained after account deletion where necessary to preserve subscription status, enforce usage limits or credit policies, prevent fraud or abuse, complete financial recordkeeping, or comply with legal obligations.

## Your Choices

Depending on how you use Snap AI, you may be able to:

- sign in with different account methods, including guest access where available;
- control camera, microphone, photo-library, and notification permissions through your device settings;
- allow or decline App Tracking Transparency on iOS, and change this any time in iOS **Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking**;
- manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple account settings or the in-app Subscription Management option (iOS only) which opens RevenueCat's Customer Center;
- withdraw AI processing consent within the app where available; and
- delete your account from within the app.

## Children's Privacy

Snap AI is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without appropriate authorization, we will delete it.

## Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information. However, no method of transmission over the internet and no method of electronic storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Third-Party Services

Snap AI may interact with third-party services, including providers for account authentication, cloud storage, AI processing, subscription and purchase management (including web checkout and redemption), Apple Search Ads attribution measurement (iOS only, via Apple AdServices and RevenueCat — only when you allow tracking), diagnostics, and customer support. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties, and you should review their policies separately where relevant.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated version on this page and update the effective date above.

## Contact

If you have privacy questions, need help, or want to make a request about your data, please contact us from within the app using the support option there.
