# Snap AI Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Snap AI app.

Snap AI helps users photograph questions, generate worked solutions, and continue learning with AI-assisted study tools. To provide that service, we process some account information, content you choose to submit, and limited technical data needed to operate the product.

We do not sell your personal information and we do not use your information for third-party advertising.

## Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

- **Identifiers and account information** such as your unique account ID and, depending on how you sign in, your email address, name, or similar account details.
- **User content** such as photos you upload, questions you type, tutor chat messages, audio you submit for transcription, saved solutions, study history, subjects, and preferences.
- **Contact information** if you choose to contact us through support from inside the app.
- **Purchase and entitlement information** such as subscription status, plan details, and whether your account currently has access to paid features.
- **Diagnostics and technical data** such as device type, operating system, app version, timestamps, IP address, crash or error details, and related service logs. Some diagnostics may be collected without being linked to your identity.
## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- create and manage your account;
- process photos, questions, audio, and follow-up requests;
- generate answers, explanations, and study tools through our backend service and third-party data-processing services;
- save your library, history, settings, and preferences across sessions and devices;
- provide subscriptions, credits, and paid features;
- respond to support requests and feedback submitted through the app;
- monitor performance, troubleshoot problems, prevent abuse, and keep the service secure; and
- comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

## How Information Is Shared

We may share information with service providers that help us operate Snap AI, such as providers that support account access, hosting, storage, billing, diagnostics, customer support, and AI or other data processing on our behalf.

We may also disclose information:

- if required by law, legal process, or valid governmental request;
- to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users, our service, or the public; or
- as part of a merger, acquisition, financing, or similar business transaction.

We do not sell your personal information.

## AI and Content Processing

When you choose to use AI-powered features, the content you submit, such as photos, text questions, chat messages, and audio, may be sent to our backend service and trusted third-party data-processing services so Snap AI can generate answers and study guidance.

You can review or withdraw AI data-processing consent from within the app where that option is available.

## Data Retention

We keep information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, maintain account functionality, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and protect the platform.

If you delete your account from within the app, we delete the main study content associated with that account from our app storage, including your profile, saved solves, notebooks, chat history, uploaded photos, and saved AI memory data.

Some limited records may be retained after account deletion where necessary to preserve subscription status, enforce usage limits or credit policies, prevent fraud or abuse, complete financial recordkeeping, or comply with legal obligations.

## Your Choices

Depending on how you use Snap AI, you may be able to:

- sign in with different account methods, including guest access where available;
- control camera, microphone, photo-library, and notification permissions through your device settings;
- manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple account settings;
- withdraw AI processing consent within the app where available; and
- delete your account from within the app.

## Children's Privacy

Snap AI is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without appropriate authorization, we will delete it.

## Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information. However, no method of transmission over the internet and no method of electronic storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Third-Party Services

Snap AI may interact with third-party services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties, and you should review their policies separately where relevant.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated version on this page and update the effective date above.

## Contact

If you have privacy questions, need help, or want to make a request about your data, please contact us from within the app using the support option there.
